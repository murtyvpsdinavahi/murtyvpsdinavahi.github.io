About Me

Dr. Murty V. P. S. Dinavahi, MBBS, PhD (Neuroscience)

Co-founder and Head, Neuroscience | Neurotech Labs, Cochin, India

I am Murty, an Indian doctor and an early career neuroscientist. I am driven by curiousity about human physiology and psychology. A keen interest in basic science and clinical/public health research has persuaded me to chose a career in academmia.

I am a hobbyist programmer with expertise in MATLAB, good knowledge of Objective-C, HTML and CSS, and working knowledge of Python.
